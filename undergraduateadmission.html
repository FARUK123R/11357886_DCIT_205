University of Ghana Admissions Information

The University of Ghana announces that applications are open for the admission of prospective applicants into various undergraduate programmes for the 2023/2024 academic year. Prospective applicants are advised to carefully read all relevant information and guidelines on the University's website before applying.

Important Points for Applicants:

  • Applicants writing WASSCE in 2023 are eligible to apply.
  • Programmes at the College of Health Sciences (except those at the School of Allied Health Sciences) are offered as FIRST CHOICE programmes only.
  • For second and third choices, applicants must choose courses from the Sciences.

Program-Specific Instructions:

Specific programmes, such as Doctor of Veterinary Medicine, Information Technology, Computer Engineering, and Biomedical Engineering, are designated as first-choice programmes. Recommendations for second and third choices are provided for certain programmes.

Application Process:

All applications must be submitted online. Visit the "How to apply online page" for detailed instructions.

Admission Criteria:

Applicants will be considered based on result equivalents provided by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission (GTEC), as per the schedule provided.
