Admissions into the Graduate Entry Medical Programme (GEMP) 2024/2025 Academic Year

UGMS Graduate Entry Medical Programme

University of Ghana Medical School

Graduate Entry Medical Programme (GEMP) - 2024/2025 Academic Year

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

  • Applicants should have a good BSc Degree (Minimum of 2nd Class Lower or equivalent CGPA in science-based courses).
  • Candidates should have obtained a good WASSCE/SSSCE grade in three (3) core and Three (3) Elective science subjects.

DEADLINE FOR SUBMISSION OF FORMS

All online applications and submission of manual forms should be completed by December 31, 2023.
